PEPPERMINT MAGAZINE SEWING SCHOOL

Hey friends! Have you got a few moments to have a yarn?

Peppermint has been doling out the DIY's and covering that creative content way back since Issue 1. Who knew that our free sewing patterns would be loved by so many of you from all over the world?! We've spent years working with so many talented patternmakers, such as Elbe Textiles, In The Folds, Paper Theory and our recent Paddington Top pattern created in collaboration with Sarah of French Navy Patterns. We're always so proud to see all of your #PeppermintPatterns come to life. With the events of last year, more and more people have been discovering their inner making maestros, and we've seen our sewing community grow exponentially.

We're delighted to have you, obviously, but far from popularity turning our heads, we want to take this opportunity to reflect and hear your thoughts on how we can be the absolute best we can be - and then get even better! And so we have a small favour to ask, dearest stichy pals. If we could borrow a few moments of your time to fill out our survey, we would be sew greatful.

In exchange for your time and input, you'll go in the draw to win one of many fabulous prizes!

One lucky respondent will win a Maker Welcome Pack from our friends at Spoonflower, including a sample pack of all the fabrics and materials they offer for printing, a copy of Spoonflower Quick-sew Project Book and a bunch of bonus goodies. Best of all, the prize includes $250 Spoondollars - website credits to purchase anything from their extensive range of custom printed, sustainable textiles.

There are also five annual memberships to the Topstitch Makers Sewcial Community up for grabs, valued at $290 each! Topstitch members can attend a variety of live/online sessions, talks with community leaders and instructor-guided sewalongs, workshops and courses.

Ten lucky people will nab a Kylie and the Machine label pack - with a fun metal tin that includes a collection of woven labels, perfect for adding the final touch to any homemade make.

So what are you waiting for?! Grab a cuppa and have your say - and you might score some sweet sewing treats along the way.
